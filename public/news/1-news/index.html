Fortune Articles from 2020/21 #

Our election forecasting prowess gained recognition in Fortune articles by Shawn Tully. Initially we suggested that prediction markets can provide better forecasts of presidential election outcomes than opinion polls. Then for the Georgia runoff elections, we showed that prediction surveys provided more accurate election forecasts than both opinion polls and prediction markets.
